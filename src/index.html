Diabetic Retinopathy Detection

Diabetic retinopathy is a retinal disease that is affected by diabetes in the eyes. The only solution for this problem is through the use of a retinal screening system that would diagnose the retinal damage at an early stage. We developed a system that will identify the features from the fundus images through CNN and eventually it will classify most-affected or least-affected eyes.
